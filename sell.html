Frequently Asked Questions

How do you determine the value of my bike?

Our valuation is based on multiple factors including current market demand, your bike's make/model, year of manufacture, kilometers driven, condition, service history, and location. We use real-time market data to ensure you get the best possible price.

How long does the selling process take?

Once you submit your details, you'll receive an instant valuation. If you accept our offer, we can typically complete the pickup and payment within 24-48 hours, depending on your location.

What documents do I need to sell my bike?

You'll need the original RC book, insurance documents, PUC certificate, and your ID proof. Our team will guide you through the transfer process and handle all the paperwork.

Is there any fee or commission?

No, there are no hidden fees or commissions. The price we offer is the amount you receive. We make our profit when we resell the bike to our network of buyers.

What if my bike needs repairs?

We buy bikes in all conditions - from excellent to needing significant work. Our valuation will account for any repairs needed, so you don't have to spend money fixing the bike before selling.
