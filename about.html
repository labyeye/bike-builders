A Message From Our Founder

Welcome to Bike Builders, where passion for two-wheelers meets exceptional service. My journey began in 2010 with a simple love for motorcycles and a vision to create a trusted platform for bike enthusiasts.

What started as a small garage operation has now grown into one of the most reputable pre-owned bike dealerships in the region. Our success comes from treating every customer like family and every bike with the care it deserves.

At Dan Electo, we don't just sell bikes - we help you find the perfect ride that matches your personality and needs. Our team of experts ensures every bike in our inventory meets the highest standards of quality and performance.

Thank you for being part of our journey. We look forward to serving you and helping you find your dream bike.
